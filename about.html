How it was created

Jordan was learning iOS programming from his cousin Alex. He got really into programming and liked it a lot. So he started learning more. His cousin's company, Pandamonia LLC inspired him to make TurtleFirst.

The Name

The love of turtles has been in Jordan's family for as long has he can remember. It got passed on to him. Jordan was driving home one night when he thought of the tortoise and the hare. The turtle got in first place. TurtleFirst!
